Before Judo

When writing about the history of judo it is important to start before the beginning of judo because judo is a modified version of jiu-jitsu.

the start of jiu-jitsu however is not clear because it is a fighting style developped by the samurai and at first also only for the samurai.

around 1867 when the feodal era in japan ended and the shogun government took seat a lot of jiu-jitsu schools were closed because the profession of samurai became a forbidden profession, this caused a lot of the old jiu jitsu masters to commit suicide and the younger masters had to find a new job.
